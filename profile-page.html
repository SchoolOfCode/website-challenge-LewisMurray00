Catherine Butler

Bio

"Hiya, Cat Butler here and this is a little bit about me. I grew up on the sunny south-west coast in Lyme Regis! I moved to Birmingham to study in 2013 and have been living and working here for 8 years, flitting from Selly Park and Bearwood."

I am a freelance producer and theatre maker and i have co-founded my own performance company LYNNEBEC in 2018 making work in outdoor spaces, festivals and site-specific spaces. I am currently developing an educational arts and science project aimed at increasing the representation of women in STEM.
